üíå What to Include in Your Message

For Technical Support

Help us help you faster by including:

  • Device: iPhone 14, Samsung Galaxy S23, Windows PC, etc.
  • Browser: Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge (and version if known)
  • Problem Description: What happened and when
  • Steps to Reproduce: What you were doing when the issue occurred
  • Screenshots: If applicable, attach images showing the problem
  • Account Email: The email you used to sign in (if relevant)

For Feedback & Suggestions

  • Describe your idea or feedback clearly
  • Explain how it would improve the game
  • Share any examples or references if helpful
  • Tell us about your playing experience and skill level

For Partnership Inquiries

  • Your company/organization name
  • Type of partnership you're interested in
  • Brief description of your proposal
  • Expected timeline and goals
