Data Deletion Policy for CrazyPuzzle

Your Right to Delete Your Data

At CrazyPuzzle, we respect your right to control your personal data. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will remove:

  • Your Facebook profile information (name, profile picture)
  • All game progress and scores
  • Leaderboard entries
  • Multiplayer game history
  • Any preferences or settings associated with your account
⚠️ Important: Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Your game progress will be lost and cannot be recovered.

How to Request Data Deletion

Follow these steps to delete your data:

  1. Via Facebook Settings:
    • Go to your Facebook Settings
    • Click on "Apps and Websites"
    • Find "CrazyPuzzle" in the list
    • Click "Remove" or "View and Edit"
    • Select "Remove App" to disconnect and delete your data
  2. Via In-Game Option:
    • Open CrazyPuzzle game
    • Go to Settings/Account
    • Click "Delete My Data" button
    • Confirm your deletion request
  3. Via Direct Contact:
    • Contact us through Facebook Messenger
    • Send a message to the CrazyPuzzle Facebook page requesting data deletion
    • Include your Facebook User ID for verification

Processing Time

Data deletion requests will be processed within 30 days of receiving your request. You will receive confirmation once your data has been deleted.

Data We May Retain

For legal and security purposes, we may retain:

  • Anonymized analytics data (no personal identifiers)
  • Records required for legal compliance (up to legal retention periods)
  • Information necessary to prevent fraud or abuse

Automatic Deletion

If you remove CrazyPuzzle from your Facebook apps, your data will be automatically scheduled for deletion within 90 days of app removal.

Questions or Issues?

If you have questions about data deletion or encounter any issues, please contact us through:

  • Facebook Messenger (CrazyPuzzle page)
  • The contact information provided in the game

Privacy Policy

For more information about how we handle your data, please see our Privacy Policy.
