Privacy Policy for CrazyPuzzle

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to CrazyPuzzle. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our game.

2. Information We Collect

When you play CrazyPuzzle through Facebook, we may collect:

  • Facebook Profile Information: Your public profile name and profile picture (if you grant permission)
  • Gaming Data: Game scores, progress, achievements, and leaderboard positions
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, and IP address
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the game, session duration, and features used

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the game functionality
  • Display your profile and scores on leaderboards
  • Enable multiplayer features
  • Improve game performance and user experience
  • Communicate with you about game updates

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Firebase/Firestore services. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Facebook Login: For authentication and social features
  • Firebase/Firestore: For data storage and real-time database functionality

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your gaming profile and scores may be visible to other players through leaderboards and multiplayer features.

7. Children's Privacy

Our game is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your data (see our Data Deletion Policy)
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us through Facebook or at the contact information provided in the game.
