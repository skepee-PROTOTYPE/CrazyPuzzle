üéÆ Gameplay Questions

How do I play CrazyPuzzle?
Click on tiles to reveal their patterns, then click another tile to find its match. If the two tiles match, they disappear. If they don't match, they flip back. Continue until you've cleared the entire board. The faster you complete it, the higher your rank on the leaderboard! For detailed instructions, check our How to Play guide.
What's the difference between Easy, Medium, and Hard modes?
Easy: 4√ó4 grid with 8 pairs (16 tiles total) - great for beginners
Medium: 6√ó6 grid with 18 pairs (36 tiles total) - balanced challenge
Hard: 8√ó8 grid with 32 pairs (64 tiles total) - expert level difficulty
Is there a time limit?
There's no strict time limit that ends your game, but your completion time is tracked and determines your leaderboard position. Faster times result in higher rankings. You can take as long as you need to complete a puzzle.
Can I pause the game?
Yes! In single-player mode, press ESC or click the menu button to pause. Your timer will stop while paused. Note that multiplayer games cannot be paused since they involve real-time competition with other players.
How is my score calculated?
Your score is primarily based on completion time (faster = better). The difficulty level acts as a multiplier, so a Hard mode completion ranks higher than an Easy mode completion with the same time. Match accuracy also contributes a small bonus to your score.

üë• Multiplayer Questions

How does multiplayer mode work?
Multiplayer allows you to compete against 2-4 players in real-time. All players get the same board configuration. The first player to completely clear their board wins. You can create a private room for friends or join public rooms for quick matches.
How do I play with friends?
Click "Multiplayer" ‚Üí "Create Room" ‚Üí Choose your difficulty level ‚Üí Share the room code with your friends ‚Üí They join using "Join Room" and entering your code ‚Üí When everyone's ready, the host starts the game.
What happens if I disconnect during a multiplayer game?
If you disconnect, you have 30 seconds to reconnect and resume your game. After 30 seconds, you'll automatically forfeit the match. Make sure you have a stable internet connection before starting multiplayer games!
Can I chat with other players?
Currently, there's no built-in chat feature to keep the game focused and family-friendly. You can use external communication tools like Discord, Facebook Messenger, or phone calls to coordinate with friends while playing.

üë§ Account & Progress

Do I need to create an account to play?
No! You can play as a guest without signing in. However, signing in with Google is recommended because it saves your scores, tracks your progress across devices, and allows you to appear on the global leaderboard.
How do I save my progress?
Click the "Sign in with Google" button. Once signed in, all your scores, stats, and achievements are automatically saved to the cloud. You can then access your progress from any device by signing in with the same Google account.
Can I play on multiple devices?
Yes! As long as you're signed in with your Google account, your progress syncs across all devices. Play on your phone during commute, then switch to your computer at home without losing any progress.
How do I delete my account and data?
Visit our Data Deletion page for instructions. You can request complete deletion of all your personal data, including scores, profile information, and game history. We'll process your request within 30 days.

üèÜ Leaderboards & Rankings

How do I get on the leaderboard?
Sign in with your Google account and complete at least one game. Your best time for each difficulty level will automatically appear on the corresponding leaderboard. Keep playing to improve your times and climb the rankings!
How often do leaderboards update?
Leaderboards update in real-time! As soon as you complete a game with a better time than your previous record, it's instantly reflected on the leaderboard. You'll see your new ranking within seconds.
Are there separate leaderboards for each difficulty?
Yes! There's a dedicated leaderboard for Easy, Medium, and Hard modes. This ensures fair competition‚ÄîEasy mode players compete with Easy mode times, Hard mode players compete with Hard mode times, etc.
Do leaderboards reset?
The main leaderboards reset monthly to give everyone a fresh chance to reach the top. Your all-time best scores are still tracked in your personal profile. Monthly resets keep the competition exciting and give new players opportunities to rank high.

Try these steps in order:
  1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)

  3. Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  4. Disable browser extensions (especially ad blockers)
  5. Check your internet connection
If problems persist, try accessing the game in incognito/private mode.

This is usually caused by:

Does CrazyPuzzle work on mobile devices?
Yes! CrazyPuzzle is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones and tablets (iOS and Android). You can even install it as a Progressive Web App (PWA) for an app-like experience. Just visit the site in your mobile browser and look for the "Add to Home Screen" option.
Can I play offline?
Single-player mode can be played offline once you've loaded the game at least once (thanks to PWA technology). However, multiplayer mode, leaderboards, and score syncing require an active internet connection.
