Our Story

CrazyPuzzle was born from a simple idea: create a puzzle game that's easy to learn but challenging to master. In a world filled with complex games requiring hours of tutorials, we wanted to offer something refreshingly simple yet deeply engaging.

What started as a weekend project in early 2025 quickly evolved into a passion project. We were fascinated by the psychology of puzzle-solving and how pattern recognition exercises can improve cognitive function. This led us to develop CrazyPuzzle with carefully crafted difficulty levels that progressively challenge players' mental agility.

Our Mission: To create accessible, engaging brain-training games that bring joy to millions of players while helping them sharpen their cognitive skills through fun, challenging gameplay.
