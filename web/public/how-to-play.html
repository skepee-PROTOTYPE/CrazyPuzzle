üéÆ Game Rules & Objectives

1

Find Matching Tiles

Your game board is filled with tiles displaying various patterns, colors, or symbols. Your goal is to find and match pairs of identical tiles.

2

Click to Select

Click on two tiles that you think match. If they're identical, they'll disappear from the board. If they don't match, they'll flip back, and you can try again.

3

Clear the Board

Continue matching tiles until the entire board is cleared. The game ends when all tiles are matched or when time runs out (in timed modes).

4

Beat the Clock

Complete the puzzle as quickly as possible. Your completion time determines your score and leaderboard position. Faster times mean higher rankings!

üí° Winning Strategies & Tips

For Beginners

  1. Start with Easy Mode: Build your confidence and learn tile patterns before advancing
  2. Scan the Entire Board First: Before making your first move, look at all tiles to get familiar with what's available
  3. Work from Outside In: Start matching tiles from the edges and work toward the center
  4. Focus on One Section: Don't jump randomly around the board - concentrate on clearing one area at a time
  5. Don't Rush: Accuracy matters more than speed when you're learning. Speed will come with practice!

For Intermediate Players

  1. Develop Memory Patterns: Train yourself to remember tile locations even after unsuccessful matches
  2. Look for Unique Tiles: Identify rare or distinctive tiles first - they're easier to match
  3. Use a Systematic Approach: Scan left-to-right, top-to-bottom in a consistent pattern
  4. Practice Daily: Regular 10-15 minute sessions improve pattern recognition dramatically
  5. Challenge Yourself: Gradually increase difficulty as your times improve

For Advanced Players

  1. Chunk the Board: Mentally divide the board into quadrants and clear systematically
  2. Peripheral Vision: Train yourself to spot matching patterns without directly looking at them
  3. Speed Matching: In multiplayer, prioritize speed over perfect accuracy (80% accuracy at high speed beats 100% slowly)
  4. Pattern Prediction: Learn common tile distribution patterns to anticipate matches
  5. Muscle Memory: Practice clicking speed and precision for faster tile selection
‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid:
  • Clicking too fast without looking properly (leads to mistakes)
  • Forgetting previously revealed tile locations
  • Getting frustrated and losing focus mid-game
  • Not taking breaks (mental fatigue reduces performance)
  • Playing only one difficulty level (variety improves overall skills)

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I play offline?

A: Yes! As a Progressive Web App (PWA), you can install CrazyPuzzle and play single-player mode offline. However, multiplayer and leaderboards require an internet connection.

Q: How do I save my progress?

A: Sign in with your Google account to automatically save scores and track progress across devices.

Q: Is there a penalty for incorrect matches?

A: There's no direct time penalty, but incorrect matches waste time as tiles flip back. Focus on accuracy!

Q: Can I customize the game board?

A: Currently, you can choose from multiple layout styles. Custom themes are coming soon!

Q: What happens if I disconnect during multiplayer?

A: You'll have 30 seconds to reconnect. After that, you'll forfeit the match.

Q: How often does the leaderboard update?

A: Leaderboards update in real-time. Your scores appear immediately after completion.
